# Nightshade Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** June 25, 2026

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Nightshade ("Nightshade," "the Bot," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects information when used on Discord servers.

By using Nightshade, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

To provide moderation services, Nightshade may collect and process the following information:

### Discord Information

* Discord User IDs
* Discord Server (Guild) IDs
* Discord Channel IDs
* Discord Message IDs
* Discord Role IDs (when required for moderation)

### Moderation Information

Nightshade may store information related to moderation actions, including:

* Warnings
* Timeouts
* Kicks
* Bans
* Reasons for moderation actions
* Timestamps of moderation actions

### Message Content

Nightshade may temporarily process message content to detect offensive or prohibited language.

Message content is only processed as necessary to perform moderation functions and is not intended to be permanently stored unless required for moderation logs or server administrator settings.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used solely for purposes such as:

* Detecting offensive or prohibited content.
* Enforcing server moderation rules.
* Identifying users who violate server policies.
* Preventing abuse and repeated offenses.
* Improving the reliability and effectiveness of Nightshade.
* Responding to support requests.

## 4. Data Storage

Collected data may be stored securely for as long as reasonably necessary to:

* Maintain moderation records.
* Prevent repeated abuse.
* Troubleshoot technical issues.
* Comply with applicable legal obligations.

Data that is no longer required may be deleted at the developer's discretion.

## 5. Data Sharing

Nightshade does not sell personal information.

Information may be shared only:

* With authorized server administrators through moderation features.
* When required by applicable law or legal process.
* To protect the security, integrity, or operation of the Bot.

## 6. Data Security

However, no method of electronic storage or internet transmission is completely secure, and absolute security cannot be guaranteed.
